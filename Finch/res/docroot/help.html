Common Troubleshooting Tips:

 

If the browser on your desktop/laptop/other device fails to connect to your iOS device try to turn off and on your WiFi connection in Settings on your iOS device.

 

If you have more trouble with Finch, please Contact Support either from the menu on the previous page or go to the support website

 

 

Useful Tips:

 

Using a Pasteboard share you can paste the URLs from your computer and open them right away on your iOS device with the 'Open Link on Device' button

 

When marking more than 1 picture as private, you can use 'highlight' gestures, instead of tapping each picture individually
